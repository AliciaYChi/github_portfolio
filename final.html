Final Project

Statement

This project is a visual-novel game coded using HTML and CSS. It's based on myself and a couple of my own self-experiences. The game represents some aspects of my life and walks through some of the thoughts and actions I sometimes think and do. It considers my worries, thoughts, and actions as I go through life as a member of the LGBTQ+ community living with anxiety.
