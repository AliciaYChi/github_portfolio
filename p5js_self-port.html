p5js Self Portrait

Statement

For this self-portrait I took the liberty to stylize it in a way that is simplistic yet pleasing. The expression I chose was the feeling I had when starting to do the coding aspect of Art-74. I was both confused and admittedly a bit overwhelmed and I wanted to capture that feeling in this piece. Although I had great difficulty working through and figuring out how it all worked it's now one of my favorite things to do.
